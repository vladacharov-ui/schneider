Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as the Policy) applies to all information that the Site Administration (hereinafter referred to as the Operator) may obtain about the user during their use of the Schneider Weisse Brauhaus website (hereinafter referred to as the Site).

1. General Provisions

1.1. User's use of the Site constitutes agreement to this Privacy Policy and the terms of processing of the user's personal data.

1.2. In case of disagreement with the terms of the Policy, the user must stop using the Site.

1.3. This Policy applies only to this Site. The Operator does not control and is not responsible for third-party sites to which the user can go via links available on the Site.

1.4. The Operator does not verify the accuracy of personal data provided by the Site user.

2. Personal Data Subject to Processing

The Operator processes the following personal data of the User, provided when filling out the table booking form:

  • User Name (for identification and personal communication);
  • Contact phone number (for booking confirmation and communication);
  • Planned booking date and time;
  • Number of guests;
  • Comments on booking (any additional requests left voluntarily).

3. Purposes of Personal Data Collection

The Operator may use the user's personal data to:

  • Identify the user to process table reservation at Schneider Weisse Brauhaus restaurant;
  • Establish feedback with the user, including sending notifications, phone calls from the administrator to confirm the booking;
  • Provide the user with effective customer and technical support in case of problems related to the use of the Site;
  • Send marketing, news, and special offers (only with the separate consent of the user).

4. Legal Grounds for Data Processing

4.1. Processing of personal data is carried out in accordance with the requirements of the Federal Law of the Russian Federation No. 152-FZ "On Personal Data".

4.2. The legal basis for processing is the User's Consent to the processing of personal data, expressed by ticking (accepting) the checkbox before submitting the booking form.

5. Storage and Processing of Personal Data

5.1. Processing of user's personal data is carried out without limitation of time in any legal way (collection, recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification, extraction, use, transfer, blocking, deletion).

5.2. Personal data of the user is stored on secure servers and is not transferred to third parties, except in cases associated with the execution of the current legislation of the Russian Federation.

6. User Rights and Withdrawal of Consent

6.1. The user has the right to obtain information regarding the processing of their personal data, as well as to request its clarification, blocking, or destruction.

6.2. To withdraw consent to the processing of personal data or delete data, the user has the right to send a written request to the Operator's email address specified in the "Organization Details" section.

Acceptance of the Consent is expressed by checking the mandatory checkbox before submitting the booking form.

Consent is given for the performance of the following actions using automation tools or without them: collection, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification (updating, modification), extraction, use, transfer, depersonalization, blocking, deletion, and destruction of personal data.

Processing of personal data is carried out solely for the purpose of reservation services at Schneider Weisse Brauhaus restaurant, communicating with the client to confirm the reservation, and providing information on the order.

5. Validity Period

Consent is valid from the moment of its provision until the processing purposes are achieved or until it is withdrawn by the User by sending a request to the Operator's email.
