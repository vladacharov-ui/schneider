SCHNEIDER WEISSE

01 / Our Concept

Bavarian Soul on
the River Don

The concept of the Schneider Weisse Brauhaus in Rostov-on-Don is directly inspired by the famous beer house of the same name in Munich.

It is headed in the sixth generation by the Schneider family. We have carefully translated the Munich spirit of hospitality and premium aesthetics to the bank of the Don River. Our restaurant is located in the central historical part of Rostov-on-Don, in a building built in the mid-19th century. The preserved original brickwork highlights the authentic view, bringing together German traditions and Don history.

02 / Heritage

The Schneider Dynasty

For over a century and a half, the Schneider Weisse brewery has remained a family business, carefully passing recipes down from father to son.

Led by the sixth generation of the Schneider family, the Munich mark has become a worldwide symbol of perfect wheat beer. Every brew is a chapter of history, crafted in strict compliance with the Bavarian Beer Purity Law of 1516 (Reinheitsgebot). In Rostov-on-Don, we are proud to offer our guests these legendary flavors paired with our exclusive gastronomy menu.

"Quality is not something you control in a laboratory. It is something you pour into the beer with your soul and the labor of generations."

— Georg VI Schneider

04 / Gastronomy

Chef Yuri Yubuzov

Munich craft culinary expertise meets local Don specialties.

Our chef Yuri Yubuzov designs unique gastronomic combinations for guests of Schneider Weisse Brauhaus. Colossal experience gained in Munich restaurants allows him to create famous dishes of German cuisine, harmonized with European recipes and local ingredients of high quality. Yuri's signature style is the perfect pairing of dishes with unique varieties of German beer Schneider.

05 / Dine In

Elegant Eclecticism

The restaurant occupies 2 spacious floors and has several rooms in Bavarian style.

The style eclecticism of wood, antique brick, metal guides, leather armchairs, and soft carpets combined with warm diffused light creates a cozy atmosphere. Old German engravings placed on all walls give the interior the atmosphere of an old gallery.
